HOW TO PLAY

Fill a 3×3 grid with MLB players that match row + column criteria.

Players alternate turns. Get 3 in a row to win.

Overtake an opponent's cell by entering a rarer answer — rarity is based on how frequently a player appears as a correct answer.

The rarer your pick, the harder to beat.
